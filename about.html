About Tadio's Towing

Locally owned and operated in Honolulu, Hawaii for over 10 years. We built this business one tow at a time.

Our Story

Tadio's Towing started with a single truck and a commitment to showing up when Honolulu drivers needed help. Over the past decade, the business has grown through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat customers who know they can count on a fast, reliable response at any hour.

We are a locally owned LLC based in Honolulu, HI 96816. Every call is answered by someone who knows the island's roads, traffic patterns, and neighborhoods. That local knowledge translates into faster arrival times and smoother service.

What We Stand For

  • Reliability: Open 24 hours, 7 days a week. We answer when you call.
  • Transparency: Service details and pricing are confirmed before dispatch.
  • Care: Licensed and bonded. Every vehicle is handled with attention and respect.
  • Community: We live and work in Honolulu. Supporting local drivers is personal for us.

Credentials

Tadio's Towing LLC is a registered carrier with the FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration). We maintain active operating authority, proper insurance, and compliance with all state and federal towing regulations.
