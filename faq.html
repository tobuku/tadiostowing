Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our towing and roadside assistance services.

What are your hours?

We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Roadside emergencies do not follow a schedule, and neither do we.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Honolulu and serve nearby areas across Oahu, including Waikiki, Kaimuki, Kalihi, Aiea, Pearl City, Kaneohe, Kailua, and more. Call to confirm coverage for your specific location.

How much does a tow cost?

Pricing depends on the type of service, distance, and vehicle. We confirm the cost before dispatching so there are no surprises. Call (808) 389-9888 for a quote.

Do you offer junk car removal?

Yes. We offer free junk car removal with same-day pickup available in many cases. Call to schedule.

What should I do while I wait for the tow truck?

Stay in a safe location away from traffic. Turn on your hazard lights. If you are on a highway, stay behind a guardrail if possible. Let us know about any safety concerns when you call.

Can you tow my car to a specific shop?

Yes. We tow to any destination you choose: your home, a repair shop, a dealership, or another location. Just let us know when you call.

Do you handle accident recovery?

Yes. We provide accident recovery services including safe vehicle extraction, loading, and transport. We can coordinate with authorities on scene if needed.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Tadio's Towing LLC is licensed, bonded, and registered with the FMCSA. We maintain proper insurance and comply with all state and federal towing regulations.
