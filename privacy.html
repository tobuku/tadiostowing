Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you contact Tadio's Towing by phone, we may collect your name, phone number, vehicle details, and location in order to provide towing or roadside assistance services. This website does not use contact forms or collect personal data through online submissions.

How We Use Your Information

Information provided during service calls is used solely to dispatch and complete your requested service. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

This website may use basic analytics to understand visitor traffic. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party sites such as review platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, call us at (808) 389-9888.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
